i love my family



My family is awesome. I have a brother, a sister and my parents. In our family we do things that you can’t afford.
I love my family but mostly my little brother because he is very cute, cheeky, funny and jumpy.
My mum is smart and she means a lot to me and my sister.
My dad goes to work and brings the money to pay the bills. Sometimes he takes us out for dinner and volleyball.
In our family we have a cheeky little girl and that is my sister O’Shianah.
She is very cheeky you will never imagine. My sister goes to day care every Friday and every time we pick her up she talks all the time all the way home.
Hi my name is Alexis I go to Westport School, I play basketball and volleyball.
I am the oldest child in my family. I am 9 years old and am cheeky too like my little sister.
At school I have only one friend and she has been my best friend ever since I started school. My family is my world because I love my family very much more than my friends.
